Dragonfly Landing

Instead of going the AirBnb, Vrbo route a couple wanted their own dedicated website to feature their short-term rental cottage in Maine.

Our goal was a stylish, lean-and-clean site that was set up to add increased functionality (blog posts, direct booking, etc) over time.

www.dragonflylandingmaine.com

Challenges

The function and layout of booking sites have become second nature to the public. So how do we incorporate the "familiar" with a more unique design that works regardless of screen size and is not bloated with content?

We started with an arial picture grabs your attention. Next the image carousel cycles through a variety of shots at a steady pace. The "stats bar" shows quick-glance, high-level infomation about the cottage and the familiar "tiles" showing details of each room, finally followed by a scroll-able picture gallery.

The end result is a unique, information rich and expandable site that stands out against template generated counterparts.
